What is FlickDupFinder ?

Ever got your photos uploaded twice on Flickr ? Wondering if you got photos more than once on your Flickr account ?

FlickrDupFinder let you easily find duplicate photos on your Flickr account.

Login, click on "Find duplicate". FlickrDupFinder will fetch your photos, analyze them, and show you a list of duplicate candidates.

FlickDupFinder is an online tool, so nothing to download or install. Just use FlickrDupFinder right away.
